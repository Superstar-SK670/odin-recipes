Butter Chicken

Butter chicken: The Greatest Indian food

Butter chicken, traditionally known as murgh makhani, an Indian dish which is a type of curry made from chicken with a spiced tomato and butter sauce. Its sauce is known for its rich texture. It is similar to chicken tikka masala, which uses a tomato paste.

Ingredients

    The following are the ingredients needed to make this dish:
  • 1 cup butter, divided
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 15 ounce can of tomato sauce
  • 3 cups heavy cream
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1.5 pounds of skinless, boneless chicken breast cut into bit-sized chuncks
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons tandoori masala

Steps

    The following are the step-by-step instructions on making this recipe
  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in onion and garlic, and cook slowly until the onion caramelizes to a dark brown, about 15 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, combine cream, tomato sauce, remaining butter, salt, cayenne pepper, and garam masala in a saucepan over medium-heat; bring to a simmer. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes. Sit in carmelized onions.
  4. While the sauce is simmering, toss chicken with vegetable oil until coated. Season with tandoori masala and spread out onto a baking sheet.
  5. Bake chicken in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 12 minutes
  6. Add cooked chicken to the sauce and simmer for 5 minutes before serving.
