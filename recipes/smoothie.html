Blackberry Rasberry High-calorie Smoothie

Blackberry Rasberry Smoothie : The perfect high-calorie smoothie for your morning

The blackberry rasberry smoothie is a fresh take on a traditional berry smoothie. Using blackberries and raspberries creates a rich but still slightly tart flavor. The tartness is balanced out by also adding natural sweetness by using honey. Plus, it has vanilla extract, which elevates any smoothie. Again, avocado is added to increase the calorie content, making the smoothie extra smooth.

Ingredients

    The following are the ingredients needed to make this dish:

  • 1 cup blackberries (frozen)
  • 0.5 cups rasberries (frozen)
  • 0.5 avacado
  • 0.5 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup oatmilk
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 0.5 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

    The following are the step-by-step instructions on making this recipe:

  1. Obtain all the ingredients and put them in a blender

  2. Blend for 2-2 minutes

  3. Take out the smoothie in a large glass and drink
